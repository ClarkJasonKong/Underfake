From you, a year ago

Last Christmas, I spent the event alone,

Sprawling on bedding, in an ill-lit room.

Reeking of desperation;
Seeping with anxieties;

No hope, can't even mention,
by rapid-fire irrationalities.

then,

You came out of nowhere,
as the last person I'd expect.

behind my friends, family,
or even my best friend.

A mere mini mesage,
made me merry, it managed.

Maybe most mistakes, must
only have moments of mileage?

One needn't be happy,
specially during a holiday.

"It's human to be melancholy."
that's what you had me say.

A year has passed,
and our positions have switched,

and all i can say,
is life is strangely a bitch.

"Are you alright?" i ask you worriedly.
You reply, "No, but i will be, eventually."

Though i wish for you to be dearly happy,
I learned from you, one also needn't hurry.

as we approach the new year,
i hope you won't think of this as lame,

but know that your friends wil be there,
wishing for you the same.

and i will be here,
waiting for your smile again.
